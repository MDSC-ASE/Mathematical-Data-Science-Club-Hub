Research

Research is one of the core pillars of the ASE Mathematical Data Science Club (MDSC-ASE). We encourage members to explore real-world problems through data-driven analysis, quantitative methods and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Research Areas

Data Science

Data analysis, visualization, predictive modeling and machine learning.

Statistics

Statistical inference, experimental design and applied statistics.

Artificial Intelligence

Machine learning, deep learning and intelligent systems.

Econometrics

Quantitative analysis of economic and social phenomena.

Mathematical Modeling

Using mathematical tools to understand and solve real-world problems.

Public Policy & Development

Applying data science and statistics to development challenges.

Current Status

The club is currently building its research capacity through training, collaborative learning and skill development. Research projects and publications will be added as they become available.

Future Research Projects

  • Conflict and Security Analysis using ACLED Data.
  • Economic Development and Data Analytics.
  • Machine Learning Applications in Social Sciences.
  • Public Health and Biostatistics Research.
  • Artificial Intelligence for Development.
