About the Club

The Mathematical Data Science Club (MDSC) is a student-led academic organization at the African School of Economics. The club brings together students interested in mathematics, statistics, economics, finance, artificial intelligence and data science.

Through seminars, collaborative projects, workshops and research initiatives, the club provides an environment where students strengthen their analytical skills while preparing for graduate studies and professional careers.

The club also promotes interdisciplinary collaboration and encourages members to contribute to impactful research addressing African development challenges.

03

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a dynamic academic environment where students strengthen their quantitative, computational and research skills through collaborative learning, innovation and interdisciplinary research.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's leading student communities in mathematics, statistics, economics and data science, recognized for academic excellence, impactful research and professional development.
