Overview

The Mathematical Data Science Club brings together students who are passionate about Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Artificial Intelligence, and evidence-based decision making. Through collaboration, research, and continuous learning, members contribute to building a strong academic and professional community.

The club was founded in 2026 by five Master's students from the Master in Mathematics, Economics and Statistics (MMES) program at ASE University.
